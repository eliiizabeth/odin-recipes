Ingredients

  • Cherries, 24 oz
  • Cane sugar, 8 cups
  • Water, 24 oz

Steps

  1. 1.Blend or finely chop the cherries until it is puree.
  2. 2. In a large bowl dissolve the cane sugar, then add the cherries.
  3. 3.Cover the mixture with water and let sit in the fridge for 3 hours until cooled.
